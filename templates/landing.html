What you can report

Four categories. One tap to file.

Photo and GPS location get attached automatically, so departments know exactly where and what they're dealing with.

Garbage & dumping

Overflowing bins, illegal dumping, missed collection.

Potholes & roads

Broken pavement, cracked roads, damaged footpaths.

Water leakage

Burst pipes, leaking mains, standing water on streets.

Street lights

Outages, flickering lamps, poles down or damaged.

How it works

From report to resolved, in four steps.

No black box. Here's exactly what happens after you hit submit.

STEP 01

You report it

Photo, category, and GPS location, filed in under a minute.

STEP 02

It's routed

Your ticket is assigned to the department responsible for it.

STEP 03

You're notified

Status updates land automatically as work progresses.

STEP 04

It's resolved

Proof-of-resolution photo attached, ticket closed, done.
